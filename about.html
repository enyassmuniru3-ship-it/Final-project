About Pawtner

Every year, millions of people look for a dog, and millions of dogs look for a home. But traditional searches usually start and end with a photo and a breed label. The problem? A high-energy Border Collie might look great on a screen, but they aren't always the best fit for a cozy studio apartment and a 50-hour work week. That's why we created Pawtner. We believe that the bond between a human and a dog is pure magic—but only when the match is right.

Our Mission

At Pawtner, we take the guesswork, stress, and text-heavy searching out of finding a dog. We look past the surface-level cuteness to focus on what actually creates a lifelong bond: true lifestyle compatibility. By analyzing energy levels, daily routines, living spaces, and personality traits, Pawtner bridges the gap between hopeful owners and their ideal canine counterparts. We aren't just helping people find a pet; we're helping them find their ultimate sidekick.

How It Works

We treat dog matchmaking a lot like modern relationship building—minus the awkward first dates.


The Lifestyle Deep-Dive: Users tell us about their daily rhythm, from their morning routines to their weekend hiking habits (or couch-lounging preferences).


The Compatibility Algorithm: We cross-reference those habits with a dog's specific behavioral needs, temperament, and stamina.


The Perfect Match: We connect users directly with the dogs that fit seamlessly into their world, ensuring a happier home for both human and hound.
